## Your Task

### Analysis Requirements
1. **Overall Sentiment**: Determine if the text is bullish, bearish, or neutral
2. **Sentiment Score**: Assign a score from -1 (extremely bearish) to +1 (extremely bullish)
3. **Confidence Level**: How confident are you in this assessment (0 to 1)
4. **Key Factors**: Identify specific factors driving the sentiment FROM THE TEXT ONLY
5. **Impact Assessment**: Which underlyings are affected and how
6. **Trading Implications**: What this means for options traders

### Scoring Guidelines
- **+0.7 to +1.0**: Strongly bullish (Fed rate cuts, strong earnings, institutional buying)
- **+0.3 to +0.7**: Moderately bullish (stable growth, dovish Fed policy)
- **-0.3 to +0.3**: Neutral (mixed signals, consolidation)
- **-0.7 to -0.3**: Moderately bearish (concerns, volatility)
- **-1.0 to -0.7**: Strongly bearish (Fed rate hikes, institutional selling, crisis)
